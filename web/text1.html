Text Analytics: An Introduction to the Science and Applications of Unstructured Information Analysis

John Atkinson-Abutridy (Author)

Text Analytics: An Introduction to the Science and Applications of Unstructured Information

Analysis is a concise and accessible introduction to the science and applications of text analytics (or text mining), which enables automatic knowledge discovery from unstructured information sources, for both industrial and academic purposes. The book introduces the main concepts, models, and computational techniques that enable the reader to solve real decision-making problems arising from textual and/or documentary sources.
